Why Hypnotherapy Works for Smoking Cessation

Most quit attempts fail not because of a lack of willpower, but because the subconscious mind still associates smoking with comfort, relief, and identity. Nicotine patches and gum can reduce the physical craving, but they can't change the mental patterns that drive you to light up.

Solution-focused hypnotherapy reaches those deeper patterns. In a calm, focused state, your mind becomes open to new associations — so that cigarettes no longer feel like a solution to stress, boredom or routine. You simply stop wanting to smoke.

My father was a lifelong pack-a-day smoker who stopped completely in his later years. That personal connection is why I developed the FULL STOP method — a structured, compassionate programme that combines clinical hypnotherapy with NLP techniques for lasting results.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many sessions will I need to stop smoking?

Many clients achieve lasting results in just one extended session (up to 2 hours). Some prefer a two-session programme. We discuss the right approach for you during your free consultation.

Will I gain weight when I stop smoking?

Not necessarily. We actively address the habit of oral comfort and stress-eating as part of the programme, so you leave with strategies to maintain a healthy relationship with food and your body.

What if I've tried to quit before and failed?

Most people who quit for good have made several attempts first — each one teaches you something. Hypnotherapy works differently from willpower or patches because it addresses the subconscious patterns, not just the physical habit.

Is hypnotherapy safe?

Absolutely. Solution-focused hypnotherapy is a well-established, evidence-based talking therapy. You remain in full control throughout — hypnosis is simply a state of focused relaxation, much like daydreaming.

Can I have sessions online?

Yes. Sessions are available via Zoom and are just as effective as face-to-face. Many clients find the comfort of their own home actually helps them relax more deeply.
