The Gap Between Potential and Performance

Most people who come to me for peak performance support aren't struggling — they're already doing well. They're athletes who train hard, professionals who work diligently, creatives who pour themselves into their craft. And yet there's a persistent gap: a sense that on the biggest occasions, under the brightest lights, something gets in the way.

That something lives in the mind. Nerves that spike at the wrong moment. An inner critic that chooses the worst possible time to speak up. A habitual tendency to play it safe when boldness is called for. These aren't character flaws — they're patterns. And patterns can be changed.

Solution-focused hypnotherapy works directly with the subconscious mind, where those patterns are held. By combining clinical hypnotherapy with NLP techniques, we build the mental habits of your best performance and make them your default — not just your occasional experience.

You may recognise these experiences

  • Knowing what you're capable of, but struggling to access it under pressure
  • Overthinking in the moment when instinct should take over
  • Post-event frustration — "I know I can do better than that"
  • Self-doubt that grows louder the higher the stakes
  • Recovering slowly from setbacks, mistakes or criticism
  • Nerves that disrupt preparation, sleep or execution
  • Difficulty maintaining consistency at the top of your game
  • A ceiling you keep hitting but can't seem to break through

Business Professionals & Leaders

Creatives & Performers

Students & Academics

How Sessions Work

Every session is built around your specific context, goals and the performance demands of your field. There's no generic template — just a thoughtful, collaborative process tailored entirely to you.

Clarifying Your Peak State

We begin by exploring what your best performance actually feels like — the qualities, the mindset, the physical sensations. Most people know intuitively when they're in flow, even if they've never articulated it. Making that state conscious is the first step to being able to recreate it.

Identifying What Gets in the Way

Using solution-focused and NLP approaches, we identify the specific patterns — thoughts, feelings, habits — that disrupt performance at key moments. We don't dwell on them, but understanding them is useful. Knowledge reduces their power.

Reframing & Building Mental Anchors

NLP techniques allow us to shift unhelpful internal narratives and install positive anchors — specific mental and physical cues you can activate before and during performance to access your best state on demand.

Deep Hypnotherapy

In a calm, focused hypnotic state, your subconscious mind is highly receptive to new patterns. We use this to embed the qualities of peak performance — focus, composure, confidence, resilience — at a deep level, so they become habitual rather than effortful.

Tools to Take Away

You'll leave each session with practical techniques — self-hypnosis, breathing protocols, visualisation practices and mental routines — that you can use independently before, during and after performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many sessions will I need?

This varies depending on your goals and how specific the performance context is. Some clients achieve significant breakthroughs in 2–3 sessions; others prefer an ongoing relationship as they move through different performance demands. We'll discuss the best approach in your free consultation.

Do I need to already be performing at a high level?

Not at all. Peak performance work is just as valuable for someone building confidence in a new area as it is for someone competing or performing at the top of their field. What matters is that you have a goal and a genuine desire to pursue it.

Is this different from sports psychology?

There is significant overlap, and the two approaches work well alongside each other. Hypnotherapy works at the subconscious level where habits and automatic responses are formed, which can complement the more conscious, analytical tools of traditional sports or performance psychology.

Can sessions be online?

Yes. Online sessions via Zoom are available and work very well. Many clients find it easier to relax and focus in the comfort of their own space. Sessions are also available in person in Reigate, Surrey.

What if I've tried other things and they haven't worked?

It's actually quite common for clients to come to hypnotherapy after other approaches haven't quite delivered what they hoped for. Working directly with the subconscious can reach things that purely conscious, rational approaches sometimes can't. A free consultation is a good way to explore whether this might be the missing piece for you.

You Already Have What It Takes

The potential is there. Sometimes it just needs the right environment and support to fully emerge. A free initial consultation costs nothing and commits you to nothing — it's simply a conversation about where you are and what you want to achieve.
